  • DM-1 The Pit

  • CTF-1 - The Pit

Game Modes

  • Death Match - Destroy your friends, destroy your foes. Players attempt to destroy one another for points towards a set goal. Players respawn on points designated on the map.
  • Survival - Death Match You Only Live Once style. Last man standing wins.
  • Team Death Match - Destroy your foes with your friends to gain points for the team.
  • Team Survival - Last team standing wins.
  • Capture the Flag - Scoring is only accomplished by acquiring an enemy's flag, and getting it back to your base. If a player is destroyed while carrying an enemy flag, it is dropped where the player was destroyed. If a player touches a dropped flag of his own team, it immediately goes back to its starting point. When players are destroyed, they respawn in their designated areas.
  • Survival Capture the Flag - Same as capture the flag, but with no respawning.
  • Death Ball - There is a ball. Points are scored getting the ball into the goal zone. Go Team! Score the points!
  • Titan - One player is randomly chosen to be the Titan, and starts off with a modification card. The Titan scores points by killing any player. Players my only score points by killing the Titan, which upon death, transfers all modifications to the player who destroyed the Titan. Any time a player becomes a Titan, they gain a modification card. First person to a set score wins.
