Movement

Characters may move up to the amount of their speed (blue) dice at any time during their turn.

Actions can be taken before, after, and during movements.

Players may also choose to make no actions during their turn, and move double the amount of their speed (blue) dice.

Characters can push or pull can other players or objects within melee range where the opposing character may choose to be allowed to be pushed or pulled, however they may also resist by rolling their speed (blue) dice against the character pushing or pulling them for each tile a player would be pushed or pulled.

Not all objects may be pushed or pulled, but if they can, they have a set difficulty required to be rolled against by the character's speed (blue) dice.

Some tiles, such as pits and stairs, can be jumped over or up by using an additional movement.
