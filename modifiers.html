Modifier Cards

These change the way the way players can interact in the game, and alter the three basic character stats, Speed, Offense, and Defense. There is either a recurring effect, or a discardable single use effect. Recurring effects are designated by a ciruclar icon, single use effects are are designated by a hand discarding a card icon.

Speed Modifications

Speed, when modified, will do things like change how much characters move, or how they move, or otherwise effect their movement.

Offense Modifications

Offense can be modified in many ways, from damage, or effects to either attacker or defender.

Defense Modifications

Defense modifications will generally change how a character's defensive roll is made, possibly adding effects as well.

Characters may also have special abilities in the form of modifications.

Modifications or special abilities do not always have to be used when possible unless the modification states so.
