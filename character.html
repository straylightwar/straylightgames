The Player Character

  • Dice Pool
    • Speed - Represented by blue dice. Determines initiative and overall movement.
    • Offense - Represented by red dice. Determines the capability to attack.
    • Defense - Represented by black dice. Determines the capability to defend.
  • Special Abilities - Alter the way movements and actions can take place.
